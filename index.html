HOW IT WORKS

Ready to Train Like A Champion, but not sure where to start?

Whether you're looking to train clients, or you're a customer looking to take your training to the next level, take a look at some frequently asked questions below to see how our personal training services work.

Customer FAQ
Trainer FAQ

What activities are available?

We currently offer training sessions in cardio(e.g. running, indoor cycling, etc.), strength training, kickboxing, and yoga. We plan on adding more activities soon, so stay tuned!

How do I schedule appointments?

From your Customer Dashboard, scroll down to the "Search For Appointments" section. Any calendar dates that are blue have available appointments. Click on a date in order to see what appointments are available on that day. If you're interested in reserving one, click on the session to see details and pay. Once you've paid, your appointment is confirmed and will appear in your Dashboard under the "View My Appointments" section.

How much do sessions cost?

The price of sessions vary by the trainer and activity because we allow trainers to control the price of their sessions. The price of an individual session can be see once you click on a date and see available appointments.

Why do you ask for the email address of the friend who referred me to TLAC?

If you refer a friend to TLAC, you may receive a special thank-you gift from us, so make sure to get the word out about how much you love to Train Like A Champion!

What activities can I train clients in?

We currently offer trainers to work with clients in cardio(e.g. running, indoor cycling, etc.), strength training, kickboxing, and yoga.

How much can I charge for sessions?

At Train Like A Champion, you are able to set your own prices for sessions of each activity type.

We do not require minimum or maximum prices; TLAC will take a fee of $15 or 20% of the cost of each session, whichever is greater.

How do I post the times I am able to train clients?

In the Trainer Dashboard, you can update your availability in the "Edit Availability" section. To start, click on a date on the calendar. Any existing available appointments (those for which customers have not signed up) will appear. From there, you can either edit the existing times by clicking the "edit" button, changing the information, and clicking "save", or you can create new sessions by entering information on an empty row and clicking "save."

Need to add additional times? You can click "add row" to add additional empty rows in which you can save new appointment information.

Why should I train clients at Train Like A Champion?

At Train Like A Champion, you have the flexibility to train clients when you want, how you want, and you can set your own prices. It's like being your own boss!

We want to let trainers do what they do best; help their clients become the best version of themselves through exercise.

We want to help you become the best version of yourself!
